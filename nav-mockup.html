Acme Technologies Inc.
Software Engineer · 2023-09-14 · Panel III
PDF
This matter involves an appeal from the denial of a PERM labor certification application for the position of Software Engineer. The employer filed a recruitment campaign consisting of job postings on the company website, job boards, and university outreach programs… The CO's denial was based on alleged deficiencies in the recruitment steps. However, the employer demonstrated that all required recruitment methods were properly documented and in compliance with 20 C.F.R. § 656.17(e). We therefore AFFIRM the CO's decision and find in favor of the petitioner.
